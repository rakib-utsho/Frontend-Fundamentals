My Faviourite Food is Curry Rich; Which made by my mom

Here is the ingidients and steps to make it:


Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. Potato
  3. Egg
  4. Onion
  5. Ginger
  6. Garam Masala
    • Cumin
    • Black and White Peppercorns
    • Coriander seeds
  7. Red Chili Powder
  8. Turmeric Powder
Stapes:
  • At First make the rice
  • Boil the Egg and Potatos
  • Add Onion, Ginger, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric Powder and Garam Masala and mix them well with egg and Potato.
  • Add Water for make the curry
  • Add rice in a bowl then add curry in this bowl

Now your curry rice is ready; Enjoy it!
